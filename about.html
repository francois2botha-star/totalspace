About Us

Total Space Solutions is a family-run business led by a husband-and-wife team who call Hermanus home. We started this company to help our neighbours and local businesses enjoy cleaner, greener, and more welcoming spaces—without the stress.

With a background in gardening, cleaning, and property care, we bring hands-on expertise and a personal touch to every job. Our values are simple: honesty, reliability, and a genuine passion for service. We believe in building long-term relationships with our clients, many of whom come to us through word of mouth.

Whether you need a once-off clean, regular garden maintenance, or urgent rubble removal, we’re here to help. We’re proud to serve the Hermanus community and look forward to making your space shine!
